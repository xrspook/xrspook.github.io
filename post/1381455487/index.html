Christian Pulled From WWE Events, Who Will Face Alberto Del Rio This Weekend?

By Raj Giri | October 10, 2013

As noted yesterday, WWE posted the promo above on their YouTube channel advertising Christian and Rey Mysterio for next week’s WWE tour in Mexico. Christian, who has been out of action since suffering a concussion last month, was also slated to face Alberto Del Rio at several upcoming WWE live events.

WWE has since pulled Christian from advertising for next week’s tour, while Mysterio is still scheduled to appear. Also, Del Rio is now slated to defend his World Heavyweight Championship against The Great Khali at this weekend’s WWE live events in Pennsylvania and Rhode Island.
