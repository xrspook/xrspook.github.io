AAA 6/14 - Arena Monterrey (Verano de Escandalo)

AAA Mega Heavyweight Title: Alberto el Patron beat Brian Cage by DQ to retain his title in a match full of interference from the referee Hijo del Tirantes, Hijo del Texano, Hijo del Fantasma, & Pentagon Jr. with Alberto also getting help from Blue Demon Jr..
