WWE SMACKDOWN REPORT: SHIELD AND WYATTS CONFRONTATION; BATISTA TALKS; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2014-02-28 20:56:22

We are in Milwaukee, Wisconsin and your announcers are Michael ‘Prince of Fielders’ Cole and John ‘The WWE Network problems were due to the black helicopters’ Layfield.

Vickie Guerrero is in the office and Alberto tells her to congratulate him for beating that perro on Raw. Vickie says that she has not congratulated Batista for beating Del Rio at the pay per view. Now they are tied one-to-one.

Alberto says that he was going to ask Vickie for a match.

Vickie says that she already put Dolph in a match with Batista.

Alberto says that he has a hot mamacita waiting for him so he will take the night off. Alberto says that Vickie’s hair looks lovely.

Sheamus enters and he says that he is looking for a fight. Why not him and Bertie have a fight. Sheamus says that it will be like a night off if he gets the match. Sheamus tells Alberto his hair looks lovely . . . fella.

Vickie tells Alberto that his match is next.

We go to commercial.

Match Number One: Sheamus versus Alberto Del Rio

They lock up and Sheamus backs Del Rio into the corner and Del Rio with punches to the taped ribs. Sheamus blocks a kick and he pushes Del Rio over the top rope to the floor. Sheamus with a flying shoulder tackle off the apron and then he sends Del Rio into the announce table. Sheamus rolls Del Rio back into the ring and Sheamus with knees to the back followed by a European uppercut.

Sheamus sends Del Rio into the turnbuckles but Del Rio sends Sheamus over the top rope but Sheamus lands on the apron. Del Rio tries for a shoulder but Sheamus moves. Sheamus with a kick and then he tries for the forearms from the apron but Del Rio stops Sheamus. Del Rio with a drop kick that knocks Sheamus off the apron.

Christian’s music plays and he makes his way to the ring while Sheamus is still down on the floor.

We go to commercial.

Del Rio with punches to Sheamus and Christian has joined Michael and John at the announce table. Del Rio with more shots to the ribs. Del Rio climbs the turnbuckles but Sheamus stops Del Rio with a shot to the back. Sheamus gets Del Rio on his shoulders and Del Rio tries to fight out of the hold but Sheamus with an Electric Chair drop.

Sheamus with two double sledges and then he hits a shoulder in the corner and a knee lift. Sheamus with a power slam for a near fall. Sheamus misses a short arm clothesline and Brogue Kick. Del Rio with a lungblower for a near fall. Del Rio misses a super kick and then he goes for a splash in the ropes but Sheamus misses and he lands on the apron. Sheamus with forearms across the chest.

Sheamus tries for White Noise but Del Rio escapes and he sends Sheamus into the turnbuckles and then Del Rio with a kick to the ribs. Del Rio with a super kick but Sheamus kicks out. Del Rio waits for Sheamus to get up and he goes for the step up enzuigiri but Sheamus moves. Sheamus puts Del Rio in the Cloverleaf.

Christian gets up from the announce table and he hits Sheamus from behind. The referee calls for the bell.

Winner: Sheamus (by disqualification)

Sheamus sends Christian to the floor but Del Rio kicks Sheamus in the ribs and he sets for the cross arm breaker but he takes too long and Sheamus escapes and hits the Brogue Kick. Christian gets back into the ring and he gives Sheamus the Killswitch.
