‘30 Years of WrestleMania’ roster for ‘WWE 2K14’ revealed at SummerSlam Axxess

BY Matthew Artus

August 17, 2013

LOS ANGELES — It’s safe to say that the roster for “WWE 2K14” is 30 years in the making.

Before a raucous crowd of SummerSlam Axxess attendees, WWE and 2K Sports took over the Nokia Plaza at L.A. Live in Los Angeles on Saturday afternoon to reveal part of the roster for the upcoming edition of the venerated video game series. While confirmation regarding which modern-day Superstars would appear in “WWE 2K14” drew much applause, the identities of the grappling greats featured in the game’s “30 Years of WrestleMania” mode left the WWE Universe feeling hungry to compete on The Grandest (Virtual) Stage of Them All.

Watch the “30 Years of WrestleMania” roster reveal | Pre-order “WWE 2K14” now!
