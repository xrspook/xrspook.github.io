12/17 WWE HOLIDAY TOUR RESULTS FROM UNIONDALE, NY
By Carlos on 2011-12-18 09:05:23
Kofi Kingston and Evan Bourne beat Epico and Primo.

Daniel Bryan beat Dean Ambrose.

Kelly Kelly and Eve beat Natalya & Beth Phoenix.

Ted DiBiase beat Jinder Mahal.

Zack Ryder beat Dolph Ziggler via DQ when Vickie Guerrero interfered.

The Big Show beat Jack Swagger (Mark Henry v.s Big Show was advertised but Henry did not appear).

John Cena beat The Miz in street fight, even after Kane interfered.

CM Punk beat Alberto Del Rio.

Biggest Pops:

Zack Ryder
CM Punk
John Cena
Kane
Big Show

Most Heat:

The Miz
Vickie
Dolph Ziggler
Del Rio
Swagger

Other Notes:

There were no " Cena Sucks” chants, which was very surprising. Cena was over well.

A Fatal 4 Way with Punk, Cena, Miz and Del Rio had been advertised but instead they changed it to the two singles matches.
