SPOILERS WWE SmackDown Results For This Friday

By Marc Middleton | February 19, 2013

Thanks to Calvin Martin for the following WWE SmackDown spoilers from tonight’s tapings in Biloxi, MS:

JBL and Josh Mathews are out for commentary. Lilian Garcia welcomes the fans.

The show opens with highlights from Elimination Chamber and RAW.

Ricardo Rodriguez and Alberto Del Rio come to the ring. Del Rio talks about Jack Swagger’s new attitude. Del Rio says this country was built by immigrants. Del Rio calls Swagger out but Randy Orton appears instead.

Orton enters the ring and says his opportunity was taken by Swagger. Orton wants a match with Swagger tonight. Booker T comes out and announces Wade Barrett vs. Del Rio and Swagger vs. Orton for tonight.

Backstage segment with Zeb Colter, Jack Swagger and Wade Barrett. Zeb says Barrett is part of the problem. Zeb hopes Del Rio and Barrett kill each other.

Alberto Del Rio vs. Wade Barrett is up next. Swagger and Zeb come out and watch the match from the stage. Barrett dominates most of the match but Del Rio gets the win by submission. Swagger and Zeb approach the ring. Swagger and Del Rio have a staredown to end SmackDown.

FULL SMACKDOWN SPOILERS FROM BILOXI, MS

By Jack Martin Jr. on 2013-02-19 22:26:07

The show opened when Alberto Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriguez came to the ring today. As you mentioned on your hotline today, they railed on Jack Swagger and pointed out that this country was built by immigrants. Alberto called out Swagger but Randy Orton came out instead. He got screwed at the PPV and he wants to kill Swagger tonight. Booker came out and said it will be Orton vs. Swagger and Alberto vs. Wade Barrett.

Alberto Del Rio beat Wade Barrett. Jack Swagger and Zeb watched from the ramp. After the match, they faced off, setting up their match at WrestleMania.

In the dark match, Del Rio beat Big Show in a street fight.

Spoilers WWE SmackDown Tapings Results

Submitted by Michael Bluth on February 19, 2013 - 10:24pm

WWE taped this week’s WWE SmackDownon Tuesday night from the Mississippi Coast Coliseum in Biloxi, Mississippi. Here are the results, courtesy of Prowrestling.net:

Lillian Garcia, JBL, and Josh Matthews made their entrances.

Alberto Del Rio kicked off the show and said it’s time for WrestleMania. He addressed Jack Swagger’s State of the Union address and said this county was built by hard working immigrants. He said America is great and Swagger is nothing but a Real Jackass.

Del Rio called Swagger out. Randy Orton’s music hit and the place absolutely popped. Orton went to talk in the ring and a Randy chants start. Orton said Swagger robbed him of his shot and he wants to take out his anger on Swagger tonight. Orton asked Del Rio to step aside for tonight.

Booker T came out and said his plan tonight is champion vs champion - Del Rio vs. Wade Barrett. Booker granted Orton’s wish by booking him in a match against Swagger.

Alberto Del Rio (w/Ricardo Rodriguez) beat Wade Barrett. Following the ring entrances, Jack Swagger and Zeb Colter came out and stood on the ramp to watch. It was the best match of the night. Del Rio won by making Barrett tap to the Cross Arm Breaker. The taping ended with Del Rio standing in the ring jawing to Swagger with the WrestleMania logo in the back.

Dark Match

Alberto Del Rio (w/Ricardo Rodriguez) beat Big Show in a Street Fight to retain the World Hvt. Championship. Big Show played to the crowd. The match ended when Ricardo hit Show with the bucket and then Del Rio put him through a table for the win.
