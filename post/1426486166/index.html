HUGE AAA EVENT POSTPONED 24 HOURS

By Kris Zellner on 2015-03-15 20:38:18

Due to extreme rain in Guadalajara for the last few hours and looking like it’s gonna last longer…AAA President Joaquin Roldan announced with the roster in the ring that the show would be postponed until tomorrow where the show will take place at Auditorio Benito Juarez in Zapopan at the same 8 PM EST start time.

Event With Rey Mysterio’s Return Delayed, Paige And AJ Lee On A New Tag Team Name, Roman Reigns

By Marc Middleton | March 15, 2015

Tonight’s AAA Rey de Reyes iPPV has been postponed until tomorrow night at 8pm due to extreme rain in Guadalajara. Kris Zellner reports that AAA President Joaquin Roldan announced the change with the roster in the ring tonight. Rey Mysterio was scheduled to make his AAA return tonight in a tag team match with Myzteziz, the original Sin Cara.
