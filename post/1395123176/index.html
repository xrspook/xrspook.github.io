SHOW RESULTS - 3/16 WWE in Dallas, Tex.: Second Detailed Report on Shield-Wyatts best match, flat Cena vs. Orton cage match, Henry says he’s not retiring, more

Mar 17, 2014 - 1:11:36 AM

WWE Live show results; March 16, 2014 Dallas, Tex.

Report by Nathan Pearman, PWTorch reader

I attended the Dallas WWE Live show at the American Airlines Center on Sunday. WWE will be coming back to Dallas on July 27 for another house show.

I would estimate around 6,500-7,000 in attendance - ringside was full, as well as most of the plaza level, although the upper levels were mostly (90 percent) empty. It was very heavy on families with small children - certainly fewer “vocal males” than at a typical Raw or pay-per-view. As has been the case recently, WWE heavily promoted the WWE Network.

Onto the results:

(1) Sheamus beat Alberto Del Rio. The crowd was really hot for Sheamus and there was pretty heavy heat on Del Rio. Good opener to get the crowd going, with both guys getting in their spots. The finish came when Sheamus hit the Brogue Kick and got the pin.

Overall, a mixed review for the show. Shield-Wyatts was by far the match of the night, and Cena-Orton did not come close in terms of crowd excitement. Much of the crowd around me, even the kids, were not into Cena at all, with a few exceptions (such as the three-year-old that cried when Cena lost). The split-roster for house shows definitely has forced WWE to draw things out more than they otherwise would, such as using Titus O’Neill in three matches and an extended Divas match with no crowd response.

Biggest Pops:

(1) Sheamus (2) Henry (3) Shield (specifically Reigns) (4) Kingston

Biggest Heel Heat:

(1) Sandow (2) Del Rio (3) O’Neil (4) Barrett
