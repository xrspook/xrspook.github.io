‘WWE 2K14’ 30 Years of WrestleMania Matches revealed

BY WWE.com Editorial Staff

September 23, 2013

In addition to the full roster reveal, 2K has also announced the match list for the “30 Years of WrestleMania” Mode in the upcoming “WWE 2K14” video game.

Hulkamania Runs Wild

  • WrestleMania I: Andre the Giant vs. Big John Studd

  • WrestleMania 2: Hulk Hogan (c) vs. King Kong Bundy

  • WrestleMania III: Ricky “The Dragon” Steamboat vs. “Macho Man” Randy Savage (with Miss Elizabeth)

  • WrestleMania III: Hulk Hogan (c) vs. Andre the Giant (with Bobby Heenan)

  • WrestleMania IV: Randy Savage (with Miss Elizabeth and Hulk Hogan) vs. Ted DiBiase (with Andre the Giant)

  • WrestleMania V: Randy Savage (c) vs. Hulk Hogan

  • WrestleMania VI: The Ultimate Warrior (Intercontinental Champion) vs. Hulk Hogan (WWE Champion)

  • WrestleMania VII: Hulk Hogan vs. Sgt. Slaughter (c)

  • WrestleMania VIII: Randy Savage (with Miss Elizabeth) vs. Ric Flair (c) (with Mr. Perfect)

  • WrestleMania IX: Bret Hart (c) vs. Yokozuna (with Mr. Fuji)

  • * Hulk Hogan vs. Yokozuna (c) immediately after this match

The New Generation

  • WrestleMania X: Shawn Michaels vs. Razor Ramon (Intercontinental Championship Ladder Match)
  • WrestleMania X: Bret Hart vs. Yokozuna (c) (with Mr. Fuji)
  • WrestleMania XI: The Undertaker (with Paul Bearer) vs. King Kong Bundy (with Ted DiBiase)
  • WrestleMania XI: Diesel (c) vs. Shawn Michaels
  • WrestleMania XII: The Undertaker (with Paul Bearer) vs. Diesel
  • WrestleMania XII: Bret Hart (c) vs. Shawn Michaels
  • WrestleMania 13: Bret Hart vs. “Stone Cold” Steve Austin

The Attitude Era

  • WrestleMania XIV: The Undertaker vs. Kane (with Paul Bearer)
  • WrestleMania XV: The Rock (c) vs. “Stone Cold” Steve Austin
  • WrestleMania 2000: Triple H (c) vs. The Rock vs. Big Show vs. Mick Foley
  • WrestleMania X-Seven: The Rock (c) vs. “Stone Cold” Steve Austin

Ruthless Aggression

  • WrestleMania X8: The Rock vs. Hollywood Hogan
  • WrestleMania X8: Chris Jericho (c) vs. Triple H
  • WrestleMania XIX: Shawn Michaels vs. Chris Jericho
  • WrestleMania XIX: The Rock vs. “Stone Cold” Steve Austin
  • WrestleMania XX: John Cena vs. Big Show
  • WrestleMania XX: Goldberg vs. Brock Lesnar
  • WrestleMania 21: John “Bradshaw” Layfield (c) vs. John Cena
  • WrestleMania 22: Edge (with Lita) vs. Mick Foley
  • WrestleMania 23: Batista (c) vs. The Undertaker
  • WrestleMania 23: John Cena (c) vs. Shawn Michaels
  • WrestleMania XXIV: Shawn Michaels vs. Ric Flair
  • WrestleMania XXIV: Randy Orton (c) vs. John Cena vs. Triple H

Universe Era

  • WrestleMania XXV: Edge (c) vs. John Cena vs. Big Show
  • WrestleMania XXV: Triple H (c) vs. Randy Orton
  • WrestleMania XXVI: Chris Jericho (c) vs. Edge
  • WrestleMania XXVI: Batista (c) vs. John Cena
  • WrestleMania XXVI: The Undertaker vs. Shawn Michaels
  • WrestleMania XXVII: Edge (c) vs. Alberto Del Rio
  • WrestleMania XXVII: The Miz (c) vs. John Cena
  • WrestleMania XXVIII: The Undertaker vs. Triple H
  • WrestleMania XXVIII: CM Punk (c) vs. Jericho
  • WrestleMania XXVIII: The Rock vs. John Cena
  • WrestleMania 29: Brock Lesnar vs. Triple H
  • WrestleMania 29: CM Punk vs. Undertaker
  • WrestleMania 29: The Rock (c) vs. John Cena II
