00:03:03 - 00:03:53：Bret Hart谈到了WWE PPV Royal Rumble 2013上他和Alberto Del Rio的后台情节。他觉得他称呼ADR为“墨西哥的Bret Hart”和ADR称呼他为“加拿大的Alberto Del Rio”这挺好，没什么不对的。Bret Hart说ADR的Rodriguez家族在墨西哥的地位就像Hart家族在加拿大。他一直都是Alberto Del Rio的大粉丝，并说ADR是A GREAT WRESTLER！Bret Hart说了他除了看好ADR以外，也很看好现在一些其他的年轻摔角手。

Published on Feb 2, 2013

Arda Ocal (@arda_ocal) of The Score Television Network and the Baltimore Sun spoke with former WWE Superstar Bret “Hitman” Hart (@BretHart) on the set of a new TV pilot in which Bret is co-starring. Bret talks about his relationship with “Rowdy” Roddy Piper and whether or not they are actually related. He also comments on his appearance at the Montreal Screwjob panel at the Royal Rumble and if he’s sick of telling that story. Finally Bret clarifies some controversial comments he recently about Triple H, whom he considers ““not even among the thousand greatest wrestlers ever.” Special Thanks to http://www.layfieldreport.com/
