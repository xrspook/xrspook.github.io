WWE RAW REPORT: TAG TITLE CAGE MATCH; BRYAN VERSUS ORTON; BATISTA AND DEL RIO TAKE IT TO THE NEXT STEP; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2014-02-03 21:07:01

We are in Omaha, Nebraska and your announcers are John ‘Where is Warren Buffett’ Layfield, Jerry ‘I am the true Royal in this town’ Lawler, and Michael ‘Where is Peyton’ Cole.

Batista is walking in the back and we go to commercial.

We are back and Batista makes his way to the ring.

Before Batista can say anything, he is interrupted by Alberto Del Rio.

Alberto says that before Batista puts everyone to sleep about winning the title at Wrestlemania, he wants Batista to talk about something. He wants to talk about their unfinished business.

Batista tells Del Rio that he doesn’t know what his deal is but he has no issue with Alberto. Batista says that coming back has nothing to do with Alberto.

Alberto tells Batista that he will tell him when he is done. While Batista was sitting on his couch for four years, Alberto says that he was here winning all of the major titles. Being famous does not make you a champion, having cajones makes you a champion.

Then Batista calls himself the Animal. To Alberto, Batista is just a little Chihuahua who is afraid of Alberto. He will hurt Batista until he hears Batista tell him not to hurt him or break his arm.

Del Rio punches Batista and then he continues the assault on Batista. Batista with a kick and he sets for the Batista Bomb but Del Rio gets to the floor. Batista wants a piece of Del Rio. He tells Del Rio if he wants it, he has got it.
