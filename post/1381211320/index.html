WWE RAW REPORT: MATCHES ANNOUNCED FOR HELL IN A CELL; NO ONE IS REALLY GONE WHEN THEY ARE TOLD TO LEAVE; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2013-10-07 23:00:58

Last night, at Battleground, we were going to get a WWE Champion after the title had been held up since the Raw after Night of Champions. That is where you would be wrong because Big Show stood tall over Daniel Bryan and Randy Orton. What is next for the title?

We are in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania and your announcers are John ‘Maybe the Penguins can use the Shield as enforcers’ Layfield, Jerry ‘Browns and Indians’ Lawler, and Michael ‘I want to be a Primanti Brother’ Cole.

We are back and Vickie Guerrero yells at a backstage worker who wanted vacation time.

Alberto Del Rio wants to know what Vickie is doing here. Vickie says that she was invited by Triple H to make a special announcement.

Alberto says that he was hoping to be named the face of WWE because he is the only champion. He was hoping for a night off, but the people want to see their champion. He hopes that Vickie will pick the proper opponent for him. He says that Vickie is the most respected General Manager in WWE History. He says that Vickie is sexy, beautiful, and intelligent. Alberto gives Vickie his towel and then he kisses Vickie on the cheek.

Alberto walks away.

We are back with a look at what Stephanie has done to fix what happened at Battleground.

Vickie Guerrero comes out, in her role as Smackdown General Manager, and she introduces Alberto Del Rio’s opponent and it is Ricardo Rodriguez.

Match Number Six: Alberto Del Rio versus Ricardo Rodriguez in a Non Title Match

The bell rings and Vickie says that she has another announcement to make. She will defend the title at Hell in a Cell. He will face John Cena. Ricardo with a rollup for the three count.

Winner: Ricardo Rodriguez

After the match, Ricardo celebrates on the announce table but Alberto knocks him off the table. Del Rio kicks Ricardo in the head and then he sends him into the ring steps. Del Rio gets a chair and he wraps it around the arm and he stomps on the chair. Del Rio applies the cross arm breaker and officials pull him off Ricardo.
