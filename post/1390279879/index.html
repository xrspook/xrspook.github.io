WWE RAW REPORT: WE KNOW WHO IS #1 AT THE ROYAL RUMBLE; BIG SHOW FACES OFF WITH BROCK; BATISTA RETURNS; MORE MATCHES FOR THE ROYAL RUMBLE; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2014-01-20 23:00:55

As is the tradition on the third Monday of January each year, we begin with footage of the I Have a Dream Speech by Martin Luther King Junior.

We are in Dayton, Ohio and your announcers are John ‘I have a dream’ Layfield, Michael ‘There’s Gonna Be A Rumble . . . Sunday Night’ Cole, and Jerry ‘You Were My Friend’ Lawler.

We are back and it is time to look at the Royal Rumble. . . by the Numbers.

It is now time to take a look back at the life of Mae Young with the video package.

Match Number Four: Alberto Del Rio versus Rey Mysterio

They lock up and Del Rio with a side head lock. Del Rio with a side head lock take down and Rey with a head scissors. Alberto escapes. Del Rio with a shoulder tackle and then he kicks Rey in the ribs and gets a near fall. Rey with a boot to the head and then he hits a drop kick for a near fall. Rey with a forearm but Del Rio with a hot shot and then he kicks Rey.

Rey moves when Del Rio tries for a splash and Rey with a kick to the chest to send Alberto to the floor. Rey with an Asai Moonsault onto Del Rio and the referee checks on both men. Rey rolls Alberto into the ring and he kicks Alberto. Rey with a baseball slide into the cajones. Rey punches Del Rio in the corner. Del Rio with an Irish whip but Rey gets his feet up.

Del Rio puts Rey in the ropes and then hits a stomp on Rey followed by punches. Del Rio with a clothesline to Rey and then he sends him back into the ring. Rey with a forearm to the head for a near fall. Alberto with a reverse chin lock. Del Rio with a tilt-a-whirl back breaker for a near fall. Del Rio with more kicks to Rey. Del Rio with a kick to the back and then he chokes Rey.

Rey with punches but Del Rio with a head butt and then he sends Rey under the bottom rope to the floor and we go to commercial.

We are back and Del Rio sends Rey into the ring steps and then he gets a near fall when they return to the ring. Del Rio puts Rey on the turnbuckles and he goes for the mask while we see footage from the App. Rey is in the Tree of Woe and Del Rio with kicks. Rey goes to the floor and Del Rio follows after him. Rey is sent into the apron. Del Rio with a bow and arrow using the ring post.

Del Rio gets a near fall. Rey with a kick and punches. Rey with an Irish whip but he hits the ring post when Del Rio moves out of the way. Del Rio puts Rey on the turnbuckles and he kicks Rey in the back of the head. Del Rio goes for a shoulder in the corner but Rey pulls himself up and Del Rio hits the ring post.

Rey with a senton to Del Rio and both men are down. Rey with a head scissors that sends Rey into the corner but Del Rio sends Rey to the apron. Rey with a forearm and then he goes up top for a seated splash and then Del Rio with a kick and he goes for the sunset flip but Rey rolls through and kicks Del Rio for a near fall.

Rey with a head scissors that sends Del Rio into the ropes but Del Rio catches the legs on the 619. Del Rio with a catapult into the turnbuckles followed by a super kick for a near fall. Del Rio tries to float over but Rey escapes. Rey counters an electric chair move by sending Del Rio into the turnbuckles and then he hits the 619 and then hits the dive but Del Rio gets his hand on the rope to stop the count.

Rey goes to the turnbuckles as he waits for Del Rio to get up. Rey goes for a wheelbarrow move but Del Rio counters and drives Rey into the mat face first and then he applies the cross arm breaker and Rey taps out.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio

After the match, Batista’s music plays and he makes his way to the ring.

Del Rio tells Batista to go. Del Rio says that he will see Batista on Sunday. Batista with a spinebuster when Del Rio tries to sneak attack him. Batista shakes the ropes and he signals for the BatistaBomb and he hits it.
