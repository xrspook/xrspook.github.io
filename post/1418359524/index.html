AAA TripleMania XXII Results 8.17.14: Alberto Del Rio & Rey Mysterio Appear

Posted by Larry Csonka on 08.18.2014

El Patron (Alberto del Rio) opened Triplemania tonight with his father Dos Caras and he gave a scathing speech about the WWE and the monster he encountered called racism but didn’t go into any other details before Konnan and his cronies came out to confront him leading to El Patron running them out of the ring.

  1. 4 Way Dance por la Copa Triplemania XXII: Perro Aguayo Jr. beat Myzteziz after a foule to win the Copa. This was another match filled with blood as Doc’s bad shoulder injury got reopened and Myzteziz juiced like crazy. Cibernetico chokeslammed Wagner to eliminate him then he got eliminated by a Mistica. After the match, El Patron came out and got into a brawl with Perrito where he used his jujigatame before Perrito ducked out. The show ended with Patron as the conquering hero.

The big news of the show actually came after the main event where Rey Misterio Jr. sent in a taped video talking about starting in AAA and then the feed went off so we don’t really know what all he said afterwards.
