ALBERTO DEL RIO RETURNS - 2/11 SMACKDOWN BRAND RESULTS FROM SAN FRANCISCO, CA
By Cohen Morrison on 2012-02-12 07:16:37
Results From San Francisco, California last night:

  • The Great Khali b. Jinder Mahal, After the match, Cody Rhodes attacked The Great Khali, then cut a promo on winning the upcoming Smackdown Elimination Chamber match.

  • Ezekiel Jackson b. Hunico via disqualification when Camacho interfered. Hunico & Camacho beatdown Jackson, then Ted DiBiase made the save, setting up a Tag Team match.

  • Ted DiBiase & Ezekiel Jackson b. Hunico & Camacho. DiBiase won by hitting Dream Street on Hunico.

  • Alicia Fox b. Natalya

  • Heath Slater came out to cut a promo on Hornswoggle, promising to take him out the next time he sees him. Hornswoggle’s music hit and he got into the ring. Instead of fighting, he called for back up, an FCW wrestler named Big E Langston.

  • Big E Langston b. Heath Slater. Cody Rhodes came out after the match again and attacked Langston and Hornswoggle, but Justin Gabriel made the save.

  • Intercontinental Championship Match: Cody Rhodes b. Justin Gabriel with Cross Rhodes to retain. The Great Khali came out for the save. Khali knocked out Rhodes and Hornswoggle went up top for a Tadpole Splash on Rhodes.

*** Ricardo Rodriguez announced the return of Alberto Del Rio. Del Rio cut a promo on his return until Sheamus interrupted. Sheamus challenged Del Rio to a Royal Rumble winners match, but Del Rio declined and decided to have an Arm Wrestling Match

Sheamus vs. Alberto Del Rio in a Arm Wrestling match ended in a no contest when Ricardo got involved. Del Rio left Ricardo behind and took a Brogue Kick from Sheamus.*

  • World Heavyweight Championship Match: The Big Show b. Daniel Bryan via disqualification. Bryan retains due to Rhodes & Jinder Mahal ran out and attacked Big Show. Sheamus then make the save.

  • Intermission

  • Main Event – Steel Cage Match: Randy Orton b. Wade Barrett with a RKO.

WWE House Show Results from San Francisco, California 2/11/12

By Calvin Martin Feb 13, 2012 - 7:14:43 PM

Report sent in by Gino D.Sheamus vs Alberto Del Rio

Del Rio came to the ring after be announced by Ricardo Rodriguez. He talked in the ring for a few minutes also trying to aggravate the crowd in San Francisco. Sheamus then came to the ring and addressed the crowd from “San Diego” oops. Del Rio went on to say he was still injured and couldn’t wrestle. So he challenged Sheamus to an arm wrestling match. Sheamus agreed to it and after going back in forth arm wrestling Del Rio stood up and left before they could finish. Sheamus was in the ring with Rodriguez. Rodriguez got down on his hands and knees and begged Sheamus not to hurt him. Sheamus then stood him up and acted like we was going to let him go, then Sheamus stepped back and delivered a bro kick, knocking Rodriguez over the ropes. After Rodriguez got up to leave Sheamus went around high fiving the crowd.

Winner: None
