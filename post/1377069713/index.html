SPOILERS WWE SmackDown Results For This Friday

By Raj Giri | August 20, 2013

Here are spoilers for this week’s episode of SmackDown from tonight’s TV tapings in Bakersfield, CA. Results are courtesy of ProWrestling.net and their reader, Taylor Gombos:

World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio defeated Christian via submission with the armbar. After the match, Ricardo Rodriguez ripped Del Rio and introduced Rob Van Dam. Christian hit Del Rio with a dropkick from the top rope and RVD followed up with a frog splash on Del Rio.

COMPLETE WWE SMACKDOWN SPOILERS FOR FRIDAY

By Matt Turk on 2013-08-21 00:57:00

World champ Alberto Del Rio defeated Christian with the armbreaker in a great match. Christian is in control early after a dive to the floor. Del Rio works over Christian, who makes comeback but is sent off the top to the floor. Great series of near falls in this one.

After the match, Del Rio got on the mic and played to the fans, then turned on them. Ricardo came out and praised the crowd in Spanish, then brought out Rob Van Dam. Christian nailed ADR with a missile dropkick. Van Dam hit Rolling Thunder and he and Ricardo hold up the World title.
