Ziggler Celebrates Birthday At WWE Event, RVD Teams With Punk & Sheamus, Cena Vs. Del Rio

By Marc Middleton | July 28, 2013

Triple H wasn’t the only WWE Superstar to celebrate a birthday on Saturday as Dolph Ziggler turned 33 years old.
