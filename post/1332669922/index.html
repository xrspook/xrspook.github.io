WWE IN SAN JUAN, PUERTO RICO LIVE REPORT

By Jose Perez on 2012-03-25 01:28:19

WWE RAW presented the second leg of this weekend’s house show tour in at the “Coliseo de Puerto Rico” in San Juan, PR. Here are the quick results from the show.

4. WWE Tag Team Title Match - Triple Threat Elimination - Primo & Epico (C) retained the straps after defeating Zack Ryder & Mason Ryan and Ricardo Rodriguez & Alberto Del Rio - First to be eliminated were Ryder & Ryan after Epico hit a “Back-Stabber” on Ryan, Del Rio sent Epico out of the ring and covered Ryan for the pin (@ 6 mins mark). After a really good back and forth, Del Rio and RR dominated Epico for a few minutes. Epico made the hot tag to Primo, who came in and immediately got the crowd involved. About a minute or two of offense, Primo got RR in the figure 4 leg lock, in honor Carlos Colon Sr who was in attendance. The crowd roar was very loud at this point. Del Rio came in and turned RR in order for him to counter the move. Epico hit the “Back-Stabber” on Del Rio and kicked him out of the ring. On his way back to the corner he turned back Primo to lock the Figure 4 tight and get RR to give up quickly @ the 16 minutes mark. Really fun match.
