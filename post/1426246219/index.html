Backstage News On Rey Mysterio And Alberto El Patron In Lucha Underground, Son Of Havoc Videos, More

By Marc Middleton | March 12, 2015

The next set of Lucha Underground tapings will be on March 21st and March 22nd in Los Angeles. These tapings will feature Rey Mysterio’s debut.

Mysterio and Alberto El Patron both reportedly have deals with Lucha Underground to where if the promotion takes off and does well, both of them are “tied in.” It’s worth noting that Lucha Underground could become the #2 promotion in the United States soon as far as viewers goes. They’ve been negotiating to be upgraded from the UmiMas channel to Univision. UmiMas airs the Lucha UG show in Spanish and is in 65 million homes, or 55.8% of homes in the US.
