WWE SMACKDOWN REPORT: ALBERTO DEL RIO GETS A HERO’S WELCOME; WE ARE DOWN TO ONE SIN CARA; ARE YOU READY FOR SOME VENGEANCE?

By Richard Trionfo on 2011-10-21 22:00:19

We are live on tape from Mexico City, Mexico and your announcers are Michael ‘Sin Humility’ Cole, Josh ‘Sin Quality Co-Workers’ Mathews, and Booker ‘Sin Captioning’ T.

We begin with the champion of the WWE and the man who is number one in the hearts of everyone in Mexico. Alberto Del Rio comes to the ring with Ricardo Rodriguez.

Del Rio soaks in the applause of the crowd and then he says that he will defend his title on Sunday against John Cena in a Last Man Standing Match. Tonight, he was scheduled to face Big Show, but that is not going to happen. Alberto has some video proof. We see footage from Monday night when John Cena hit Alberto in the head with the steel steps.

Alberto says that it was his dream to come to Mexico to compete for everyone. To compete for ‘his Mexico’. John Cena robbed him and the fans of that opportunity. Alberto says that he is going to make John Cena pay. He will walk into the pay per view as the champion and he will walk out as champion. He says that he is the biggest hero in Mexican history.

Teddy Long comes out to ruin Alberto’s fiesta. Teddy says that Alberto was checked out by WWE physicians and they said that Alberto was cleared to compete. Teddy says that Alberto is trying to weasel his way out of the match with Show. Alberto claims that he wants to compete in front of these people. Teddy says that Alberto is afraid to wrestle Big Show.

Alberto tells Teddy that he will make Big Show tap tonight after calling Teddy a dog.

Teddy asks the people if they want to see Alberto face Big Show and they appear to want that match. Teddy tells Alberto to get ready for his match.

Teddy goes to the back but Mark Henry makes his way to the ring.

We are back and we see footage of Sheamus’ match against Alberto Del Rio last week and what Christian did after the match ended.

Big Show is in the back getting ready for his match against Alberto Del Rio.

We go to commercial.

Match Number Five: Alberto Del Rio with Ricardo Rodriguez versus Big Show

Show goes after Del Rio and he sends Del Rio to the mat. Show with a chop in the corner and then he slams Del Rio. Show with a head butt to Del Rio and Del Rio tries to kick Show from the mat. Del Rio with a kick and punch but Show with a biel before choking Del Rio in the ropes. Show with punches in the corner and the referee warns him.

Show with another punch to the midsection. Show chokes Del Rio with his boot against the ropes and Del Rio rolls to the floor. Ricardo checks on Del Ro and Show pulls Del Rio from the floor to the apron. Del Rio drops Show on the top rope but when Del Rio goes up top, Show with a chop to Del Rio.

We go to commercial.

Del Rio with kicks and then Show sends Del Rio to the mat after blocking a kick. Show clotheslines Del Rio over the top rope to the floor. Del Rio drop kicks the steps and Show grabs his knees.

Show returns to the ring and he slams Del Rio. Show misses a splash into the corner but he is able to stop short. Show with a clothesline to Del Rio. Show with a modified Cobra Clutch on Del Rio. Show with a leg drop and he gets a near fall. Del Rio with a drop kick to the knee followed by a series of kicks to keep Show down.

Del Rio with a kick to the midsection and then he grabs the arm and he hits a single arm breaker but Del Rio can only get a near fall. Show blocks a kick and he chops Del Rio. Show with a series of clotheslines followed by an Irish whip and a running butt splash and a running shoulder tackle. Show signals for the choke slam and he grabs Del Rio but Alberto escapes and he clips Show.

Del Rio applies the cross arm breaker and Show reaches for the ropes but Show is able to get to his knees but Del Rio adjusts the hold. Show picks up Del Rio and tosses him across the ring. Both men are down in the center of the ring. Show grabs Del Rio by the throat and Ricardo Rodriguez jumps on Show’s back and the referee calls for the bell.

Winner: Big Show by disqualification

After the match Show throws Ricardo off his back and then he connects with a punch to Ricardo and then one to Del Rio.

Mark Henry makes his way to the ring and Henry runs into a punch from Show and he is out.
