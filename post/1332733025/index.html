Alberto Del Rio Speaks On Whether He Will Work WrestleMania 28

Posted by Ashish on 03.25.2012

Will he participate?

During a recent media appearance promoting WWE’s live show in Puerto Rico, Alberto Del Rio said that he will not be involved with WrestleMania 28. He said the original plan was for him to face Randy Orton at the show, but those plans got scrapped after his injury. He said there was another plan to have him be a member of Team Johnny at the show, but that those plans have also been dropped.

NoDQ.com > WWE > News from WWE in PR: Del Rio & Rodriguez Team Up

Posted by Steve Carrier on 03/25/2012 at 08:54 AM

- Alberto Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriguez teamed up at last night’s WWE RAW live event in San Juan, Puerto Rico. They lost to WWE Tag Team Champions Primo & Epico plus the team of Zack Ryder & Mason Ryan in an Elimination Match.

After Ryan and Ryder were eliminated early on, the tag champions and the team of Del Rio & Rodriguez put on what was described as a solid, fun match.

- Other notable matches at the San Juan event saw R-Truth defeat FCW star Leo Kruger, “Ryback” Skip Sheffield squash Alex Riley and Brodus Clay defeat The Miz in an eight-minute match.

- WWE did not bring their new live event set to Puerto Rico for the shows.

NoDQ.com > WWE > Alberto Del Rio Pulled from WrestleMania 28 Match?

Posted by Steve Carrier on 03/25/2012 at 08:56 AM

Alberto Del Rio was doing media for WWE in Puerto Rico this weekend and said that he is under the impression that he will not be performing at WrestleMania 28.

Del Rio said at one point, he was going to face Randy Orton and most recently plans called for him to be on Team Laurinaitis. Del Rio said he had just been told he’s no longer in the Team Laurinaitis vs. Team Long match.
