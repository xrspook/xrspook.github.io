WWE SMACKDOWN REPORT: WWE DANCES INTO NIGHT OF CHAMPIONS; THE CUTTING EDGE IS BACK; A NEW INTERVIEWER; NEW MATCHES FOR SUNDAY; AND SOMEONE TELLS YOU NOT TO BUY THE PAY PER VIEW

By Richard Trionfo on 2013-09-13 21:59:42

We are in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada and your announcers are Michael ‘Jason X is my favorite’ Cole and John ‘Takes Manhattan’ Layfield.

We go to Vickie’s office and Ricardo Rodriguez enters Vickie’s office because he was told by a runner that she wanted to speak to him. Vickie says that in the interest of what is best for business, Ricardo will not be in RVD’s corner at Night of Champions. She says that she fears for Ricardo’s safety. Since they are in Canada, they speak in Spanish.

Vickie says that if he wants to be in Rob’s corner on Sunday, Rob can be in Ricardo’s corner tonight when Ricardo faces Alberto Del Rio.

Match Number Three: Alberto Del Rio versus Ricardo Rodriguez (with Rob Van Dam)

Del Rio with a kick and punches to Ricardo. Del Rio has something to say to Van Dam and then he runs Ricardo into the corner and connects with shoulders followed by a step up enzuigiri. Del Rio with a boot to the back of the head. Del Rio mocks the Van Dam thumbs deal with ADR. Ricardo with a drop kick and a rollup for a near fall.

Del Rio with a super kick and Ricardo is down. Del Rio with head butts to Ricardo and the referee warns him. Ricardo kicks Del Rio in the head and then he runs into boots from Ricardo. Ricardo with a tornado DDT for a near fall. Rob tells Ricardo to go up top but Del Rio crotches Ricardo on the turnbuckles and then he hits a baseball slide to take care of Van Dam. Del Rio with an inverted superplex for the three count.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio

After the match, Del Rio puts Ricardo in the cross arm breaker. Van Dam comes into the ring and Del Rio releases the hold. Van Dam with a thrust kick and he punches Del Rio. Van Dam hits a five star frog splash and then he shows Del Rio how to work the thumbs.
