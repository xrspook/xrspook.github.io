SmackDown Five-Point Preview: Nov. 8, 2013

BY Michael Burdick & Joseph Naddeo

November 08, 2013

Who will look to survive against The Champ?

If all that wasn’t enough, WWE.com has learned that World Heavyweight Champion John Cena will also be back on SmackDown in some capacity tonight.

On the heels of explosive back-to-back Six-Man Tag Team Match victories alongside WWE Tag Team Champions Cody Rhodes & Goldust on both SmackDown and Raw last week, could The Champ now be ready to turn his focus to his yet-to-be-named No. 1 contender for the upcoming Survivor Series pay-per-view?

And who might that opponent be? Former titleholder Alberto Del Rio still has a contractually guaranteed title rematch and has looked as vicious as he does focused since losing the gold at WWE Hell in a Cell. On the other hand, the always-dangerous Damien Sandow made it his mission to end the Cena Era one week ago. In addition, a number of other hungry Superstars such as Big E Langston have been stepping up to the plate lately in a big way.

Who will emerge to challenge The Champ? Find out tonight, only on Syfy!
