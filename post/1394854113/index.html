WWE SMACKDOWN REPORT: TRIPLE H TALKS ABOUT DANIEL BRYAN; KANE TRIES TO SHOW HIS AUTHORITY; MORE NAMES FOR THE ANDRE THE GIANT MEMORIAL BATTLE ROYAL

By Richard Trionfo on 2014-03-14 21:00:32

We start off with a look back at Daniel Bryan standing up to authority on Raw.

We are in Little Rock, Arkansas and your announcers are Michael ‘Is today the Rock’s favorite day of the year’ Cole and John ‘Clinton Country’ Layfield.

We are back and Alberto Del Rio was in the Goldman Box talking about how he was won them all. He will have his Wrestlemania moment when he wins the Andre the Giant Memorial Battle Royal.

Match Number Six: Dolph Ziggler versus Alberto Del Rio

They lock up and Del Rio with a side head lock and Del Rio holds on when Ziggler tries to Irish whip him. Del Rio with kicks to Ziggler. Ziggler holds on to the ropes and then he back drops Del Rio to the apron. Ziggler drop kicks Del Rio off the apron to the floor. Ziggler sends Del Rio into the apron and then back into the ring.

Ziggler with a series of elbow drops, ending with the hesitation leaping elbow drop. Ziggler gets a near fall. Del Rio with an Irish whip but Ziggler gets his knees up and Del Rio puts Ziggler in the ropes and Del Rio with a double stomp as we go to commercial.

We are back and Ziggler with a cross body and he punches Del Rio. Ziggler leaps into the corner and he punches Del Rio. Ziggler with a neck breaker for a near fall. Del Rio with a tilt-a-whirl back breaker for a near fall. Del Rio waits for Ziggler to get up and Del Rio tries for a super kick but Ziggler moves. Ziggler with a DDT for a near fall.

Del Rio with an arm breaker and then he hits a super kick for a near fall. Del Rio signals for the cross arm breaker but Ziggler avoids it. Del Rio misses an enzuigiri in the corner and he gets a near fall with a Fameasser. Ziggler goes up top but Del Rio punches Ziggler in the midsection and Ziggler falls on the turnbuckles. Del Rio with an inverted superplex for a near fall.

Del Rio sets for the cross arm breaker but Ziggler avoids it and he hits the Zig Zag for the three count.

Winner: Dolph Ziggler

After the match, Renee Young interviews Dolph Ziggler about his two victories over Alberto Del Rio. She asks him if he has hit his stride. Dolph says that he is excited because we are a few weeks away from the biggest Wrestlemania of all time. Dolph says that he is the man who officially puts his name in the Andre the Giant Memorial Battle Royal. He has stolen the show on Raw, Smackdown, and pay per views before, but this is Wrestlemania. Dolph says that he makes his own destiny. When he wins at Wrestlemania XXX, he will finally have his Wrestlemania moment.
