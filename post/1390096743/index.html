Rey Mysterio Vs. Alberto Del Rio At WrestleMania?, The Bella Twins On The Red Carpet, More

By Marc Middleton | January 18, 2014

Rey Mysterio told WWE Magazine in their latest issue that his dream WrestleMania match would be against Alberto Del Rio. Rey said:

“If I were to pick a dream match-up for sometime in the future, it would be against him (Del Rio) and I would definitely put my mask on the line. I want it to be a classic lucha libre style match, mask vs. hair.”

WWE News: Rey Mysterio Wants to Face Alberto Del Rio at WrestleMania, NXT Diva Changes Her Look

Posted by Jeffrey Harris on 01.18.2014

In a new interview for the February 2014 issue of WWE Magazine, Rey Mysterio said he wants to wrestle Alberto Del Rio at WrestleMania 30 in a Mask vs. Hair match. Below are some highlights:

“Ever since the moment that Alberto Del Rio came into the company, he’s been trying to push me aside to a certain degree. I almost feel like he wants to take over the Hispanic fanbase, which is something I feel would be impossible at this point. If I were to pick a dream [WrestleMania] match-up … it would be against him, and I would definitely put my mask on the line. I want it to be a classic lucha libre-style match, Mask vs. Hair.”

http://www.411mania.com/wrestling/news/310306

¿Rey Mysterio vs Alberto Del Río en Wrestlemania XXX?

Poco a poco sigue tomando forma la cartelera de Wrestlemania XXX, el mayor pague por ver del año para la WWE, que este 6 de abril 2014, desde el Mercedes-Benz Superdome, en la ciudad de Nueva Orleans, estará celebrando su 30 aniversario, como siempre se desatan una gran cantidad de especulaciones de quien debe participar en tan magno evento.

Rey Mysterio dijo a WWE Magazine en su último número que su combate de ensueño para WrestleMania sería contra Alberto Del Río. Rey dijo:

“Si tuviera que elegir un combate soñado por algún tiempo en el futuro, sería en contra de él (Del Rio), y sin duda poner mi máscara en juego. Yo quiero que sea un combate de lucha libre de estilo clásico, la máscara vs la cabellera”.
