WWE SMACKDOWN REPORT: THE FUTURE OF THE SMACKDOWN GM; ROB VAN DAM RETURNS; THE SHIELD RE-ESTABLISH DOMINANCE; PAUL HEYMAN EXPLAINS HIS ACTIONS . . . AGAIN

By Richard Trionfo on 2013-07-19 22:00:43

We are in Providence, Rhode Island and your announcers are John ‘What’s the Best Way’ Layfield and Michael ‘We passed 100 Dunkin Donuts from the airport to the arena’ Cole.

Teddy Long is in the ring and he has an announcement to make. He welcomes everyone to Smackdown. He says that there is a new General Manager on Raw, Brad Maddox. He doesn’t know too much about Brad’s qualifications but they have to be better than Vickie Guerrero. Mr. McMahon knows what he is doing. Speaking of Mr. McMahon, he is here tonight. Rumor has it that Vince is going to be giving him a job evaluation.

Teddy asks everyone if he is doing a good job and then he asks if people want to see him as permanent General Manager.

Teddy is interrupted by the music of the real General Manager of Smackdown, Booker T and he makes his way to the ring and Teddy is shut up by the shock.

Booker asks how everyone is doing and he thanks Teddy for filling in for him in his absence and he tells Teddy that he did a hell of a job. Booker says that he is back and he is ready to do his GM duties. He asks if everyone is ready to dig.

Vince McMahon’s music plays and he comes to the ring. Vince shakes Booker and Teddy’s hands and then he wants everyone to welcome Booker and Teddy.

Vince says that he wants to make sure that there is no dissension between the two of them because only one person can be in charge. Vince asks Teddy how he would impress everyone tonight.

Teddy suggests Curtis Axel versus Chris Jericho for the Intercontinental Championship.

Vince asks Booker what he would do.

Booker says that he would make a match between Alberto Del Rio going one on one with the winner of the All Star Ladder match, Randy Orton.

Vince says that he has a tough decision and then Raw’s music plays and Brad Maddox makes his way to the ring and he is a bit disheveled. Brad wants to have a word with Vince. He says that Vince made him general manager of Raw, but he should also make him general manager of Smackdown. Brad says that he would have suggested both matches and he says that tonight is the Smackdown return of Rob Van Dam.

Vince asks Brad if he is sure about that.

Vince says that the permanent General Manager should be . . .

VICKIE GUERERO

Vickie makes her way to the ring and she hugs Vince.

Vickie takes the mic and she thanks Mr. McMahon. She says the people are the reason why she was removed from her job on Raw. That is why she hates everyone. Vickie says that she is back and she is back with power. There is nothing that anyone in this ring can do. There is nothing that anyone out there can do about this.

Vickie says that she is the permanent General Manager of Smackdown.

Vickie leaves the ring while Brad, Booker, and Teddy argue in the ring.

Match Number Five: Randy Orton versus Alberto Del Rio in a Non Title Match

They lock up and Orton with head butts and punches. Orton with punches in the corner and then he hits a standing drop kick. Orton puts Del Rio on the top turnbuckle but Del Rio with a cross arm breaker in the ropes and then he returns to the ring and he sends Orton shoulder first into the ring post.

Del Rio with a running kick to the head and then he stomps on Orton. Del Rio with a divorce court for a near fall. Orton goes to the ropes and Del Rio wraps Orton’s injured arm in the ropes. Del Rio with a kick and he applies an arm bar. Orton with punches but Del Rio with an Irish whip. Orton avoids Del Rio when he charges into the corner and he gets a rollup for a near fall. Del Rio with another kick to the head and then he punches Orton against the ropes.

Del Rio goes to the floor but Orton with punches followed by a back drop driver onto the ringside barrier. Del Rio with an Irish whip into the ring steps. Del Rio sends Orton back into the ring and Del Rio goes up top and he waits for Orton to turn around and when Del Rio comes off the turnbuckles, Orton with a drop kick.

Orton with two clotheslines and he avoids a clothesline from Del Rio. Del Rio holds on to the ropes to avoid the power slam and Orton falls to the mat. Del Rio applies the cross arm breaker. Orton gets to the ropes and Del Rio releases the hold. Del Rio with kicks to the injured arm. Orton with a back breaker but he uses the injured arm to hold Del Rio and he hesitates before making the cover.

Orton sets for the IEDDT but Del Rio gets back into the ring and he sends Orton over the top rope. Orton lands on the apron but Del Rio with a drop kick and Orton falls to the floor. Del Rio sends Orton back into the ring and he gets a near fall. Del Rio with a running kick to the injured shoulder.

Del Rio taunts Orton and mocks him by punching the mat. Del Rio tries for an RKO of his own and Orton escapes and hits a power slam for a near fall. Del Rio with a single arm breaker and then he charges at Orton and Orton sends Del Rio to the apron for an IEDDT. Orton looks around and he twists to the mat and pounds it. Orton sets for the RKO but Del Rio with a lungblower for a near fall.

Del Rio signals for the cross arm breaker and he floats over but Orton is able to escape. Del Rio misses a super kick and Orton with the RKO for the three count.

Winner: Randy Orton
