WWE SMACKDOWN REPORT: IS THE MAIN EVENT FORESHADOWING WHAT WILL HAPPEN ON SUNDAY?; PAUL HEYMAN ADDRESSES CM PUNK; BRAY WYATT SPEAKS; HAVE THE SHIELD MET THEIR MATCH THIS TIME?; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2013-08-16 22:00:52

We are in San Jose, California and your announcers are Michael ‘SummerFest is coming’ Cole and John ‘I would climb a mountain to wrestle at SummerSlam’ Layfield.

We are back and we see Damien Sandow almost cash in last week on Smackdown, until Cody Rhodes rained on Sandow’s parade.

Match Number One: Damien Sandow (with his handcuffed briefcase Version 2.0) versus Christian

Sandow with a kick and punches. Sandow with a belly-to-back suplex attempt but Christian lands on his feet and he sends Sandow into the ropes with a drop toe hold. Christian slingshots to the floor and Sandow moves. Sandow misses an elbow drop and then Christian goes to the turnbuckles and hits a missile drop kick and gets a near fall.

Sandow with punches and then he drops Christian on the top rope and hits a running knee lift and Christian falls to the floor. Sandow rolls Christian back into the ring and he gets a near fall. Sandow with kicks to the ribs and he applies a waist lock. Christian gets to his feet but Sandow with a forearm to the back and he sends Christian to the floor.

Sandow rolls to the floor and he sends Christian into the ring steps and Christian hits them with his knees. We go to commercial.

We are back and Sandow with a reverse chin lock. Christian with punches but Sandow with a knee and then Sandow trips Christian and he connects with knees while Christian hangs over the apron. Sandow stands on Christian’s chest and then he returns to the ring. Christian with a shoulder and he tries for a sunset flip but Sandow rolls through.

Christian with a back body drop and then he climbs the turnbuckles for punches. Christian with an inverted DDT for a near fall. Christian goes up top and misses a frog splash. Sandow with a running flipping snap neck breaker for a near fall. Sandow misses a charge into the corner and Christian with a back heel kick and a sunset flip from the turnbuckles for a near fall.

Christian with a punch followed by a back elbow from the turnbuckles. Sandow escapes a Killswitch attempt and then he does a cartwheel. Sandow sends Christian throat first into the ropes and then he hits the side Russian leg sweep. Sandow sets for the Elbow of Disdain but Christian rolls out of the way and then Christian with the three count thanks to a small package.

Winner: Christian

After the match, Sandow tries to attack Christian and Christian moves. Christian with a spear to Sandow. While Sandow rolls to the floor, Christian does not see Alberto Del Rio come to the ring and attack him.

Del Rio tells Christian he will never win the title. He will never be World Champion again. Del Rio hits Christian with the mic and he tries for the float over into the Cross Arm Breaker but Christian counters with the Killswitch.
