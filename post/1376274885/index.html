Reasons Alberto Del Rio Attacked His Ring Announcer

There are two reasons Alberto Del Rio attacked Ricardo Rodriguez during the August 5 edition of WWE Raw.

Word is WWE felt Del Rio wasn’t over enough as a heel and that the attack on Rodriguez would help remedy that because fans love the ring announcer. The attack was also a form of discipline for Rodriguez, who has backstage heat for openly complaining about his wellness policy violation.
