WWE Raw 2013-02-18 非官方战报

2013-02-19
DESTINY_IS_REAL
约 822 字 预计阅读 2 分钟

COMPLETE WWE RAW REPORT: NEXT WEEK WE SEE WHO FACES ROCK AT WRESTLEMANIA; WE HAVE A NEW TITLE BELT; DOLPH ALMOST CASHES IN . . . AGAIN; THE SHIELD IN ACTION; ARE THE TAG CHAMPS IN TROUBLE; ZEB COULTER TELLS US THE WORLD TITLE IS FOR THE FUTURE OF AMERICA; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2013-02-18 20:09:24

Last night at the Elimination Chamber, did the Rock retain the WWE Title to set up the Once in a Lifetime . . . for the Second Time match against John Cena at Wrestlemania? Was Alberto Del Rio able to retain against the pissed off Big Show? Did Dolph Ziggler cash in his Money in the Bank briefcase or did he tease us one more time? Did the ‘super team’ of John Cena, Ryback, and Sheamus finally send a message to The Shield? Who won the Elimination Chamber to face the World Champion at Wrestlemania 29? These questions and more will be answered on the next episode of Soap . . . I mean Monday Night Raw.

We are in Lafayette, Louisiana and your announcers are Michael ‘Why aren’t we telling people the city we are in this week’ Cole and Jerry ‘Doesn’t JBL remind you of Burt Campbell’ Lawler.

The first step on the Road to Wrestlemania tonight is made by John Cena who looks pretty happy considering he is in the main event of Wrestlemania even though he fell short against the Shield.

John says that they are in the right place because the Road to Wrestlemania is heating. They announced last year that Wrestlemania would be in the shadow of New York at MetLife Stadium. The sign above the ring means something now because Wrestlemania is taking shape. We know the two title matches. Jack Swagger will face Alberto Del Rio for the World Title. On April 7th, John Cena reminds us that he will try to become the WWE Champion when he faces The Rock.

Match Number Three: Alberto Del Rio (with Ricardo Rodriguez) versus Dolph Ziggler (with Big E Langston and AJ Lee)

Ziggler with a kick and drop kick followed by punches. Del Rio with a back heel kick but Ziggler with a kick. Del Rio sends Ziggler to the apron and Del Rio with a step up enzuigiri and Ziggler falls awkwardly to the floor and we go to commercial.

We are back and Ziggler is back in control and he chokes Del Rio in the ropes. Ziggler with a drop kick and he gets a near fall. Ziggler chokes Del Rio in the ropes and then he talks to the referee while Langston punches Del Rio. Ziggler gets a near fall and then he applies a reverse chin lock. We see Zeb Coulter and Jack Swagger watching in the back on the monitor.

Del Rio with punches but Ziggler with a knee to the midsection. Ziggler with a Fameasser for a near fall. Ziggler goes up top but Del Rio hits the ropes and Ziggler is crotched. Del Rio with a round kick to the back and then he goes for a reverse superplex and both men are down. Ziggler punches Del Rio but Del Rio punches back. They go back and forth until they go with kicks. Ziggler misses a clothesline while Del Rio hits two. Ziggler misses another clothesline and Del Rio with a tilt-a-whirl back breaker. Del Rio with a super kick and he gets a near fall.

Del Rio signals for the cross arm breaker but he takes too long and Ziggler with a swinging neck breaker for a near fall. Del Rio with an Irish whip but Ziggler gets his feet up. Del Rio puts Ziggler in the ropes and connects with a series of forearms to the lower back and he follows with a lungblower for a near fall because Ziggler gets his hand on the rope.

Del Rio signals for the cross arm breaker and Langston gets on the apron. Del Rio with a drop kick to Langston to knock him off the apron. Ziggler tries for a DDT but Del Rio gets free and he applies the cross arm breaker and Ziggler taps out.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio

After the match, Langston with a running body block on Del Rio and then he gets Del Rio up for the Big Ending and he hits it.

Ziggler grabs the briefcase and he gives it to the referee steals the contract and goes to the back. Langston forces Ricardo to drop the case. AJ gets the case and gives it to Dolph but Alberto with an enzuigiri.
