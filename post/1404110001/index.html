WWE SMACKDOWN REPORT: WHO HAS THE ADVANTAGE GOING INTO MONEY IN THE BANK?; WHAT NEW MATCHES WERE ANNOUNCED?; IS THERE BAD NEWS COMING?

By Richard Trionfo on 2014-06-27 22:00:50

We start off with a video package showing Daniel Bryan’s comments about how hard the path to becoming the WWE Champion was and he talks about how he needs neck surgery. We go to the announcement by Stephanie McMahon stripping Daniel of the WWE Championship and the announcement of the ladder match to determine the new WWE Champion.

We are in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania and your announcers are Michael ‘Parker’ Cole and John ‘Stevens’ Layfield.

Match Number Four: Dolph Ziggler and Rob Van Dam versus Cesaro (with Paul Heyman) and Alberto Del Rio

Del Rio and Van Dam start things off and they lock up. Del Rio with a side head lock and shoulder tackle. Del Rio backs Van Dam into the corner and he connects with a kick and punches. Van Dam with an Irish whip and Van Dam with a monkey flip. Van Dam gets a near fall. Van Dam with a forearm in the corner followed by a spinning heel kick in the corner.

Ziggler tags in and he punches Del Rio. Ziggler with a snap mare and then he hits a leaping elbow drop and he gets a near fall. Del Rio with an elbow and then he sends Ziggler into the air and down to the mat. Cesaro tags in and he kicks Ziggler. Cesaro stands on the head and he pulls Ziggler’s arm before stomping on the head.

Cesaro sends Ziggler into the turnbuckles and Del tags in and he connects with a step up enzuigiri for a near fall. Cesaro tags back in and he kicks Ziggler. Ziggler with a cross body. Ziggler leaps into the corner and he punches Cesaro. Ziggler with a neck breaker. Ziggler misses a Fameasser and Cesaro sends Ziggler into the corner. Ziggler moves and Cesaro hits the post with his shoulder. Ziggler with a Fameasser for a near fall.

Ziggler goes for a sleeper and Del Rio makes the tag. Ziggler with a drop kick to Cesaro but Del Rio with a clip to the back of the leg and then Del Rio with a super kick that forces Ziggler to the floor.

We go to commercial.

Del Rio goes to the floor and he sends Ziggler into the ringside barrier. They return to the ring and Del Rio gets a near fall. Cesaro tags in and he kicks Ziggler and gets a near fall. Cesaro with a reverse chin lock on Ziggler. Cesaro with a side slam and he gets a near fall. Cesaro slaps Ziggler in the head.

Del Rio tags in and he punches Ziggler. Del Rio mocks the Van Dam thumbs and Del Rio with a snap mare and drop kick to the back and Del Rio gets a near fall. Del Rio with a reverse chin lock.

Ziggler with punches but Ziggler with a sunset flip for a near fall. Del Rio with a tilt-a-whirl back breaker. Del Rio gets a near fall. Del Rio goes up top and he hits a forearm to the back. Cesaro tags in and he kicks Ziggler in the head. Cesaro with a punch and he gets a near fall. Cesaro with a reverse chin lock.

Ziggler with a punch and Ziggler with a sleeper but Cesaro backs Ziggler into the corner. Ziggler with an elbow to Del Rio on the apron and then he punches Cesaro. Ziggler with a kick and then he back body drops Cesaro over the top rope to the floor. Del Rio rolls Cesaro into the ring to make the tag.

Van Dam tags in and he connects with clotheslines followed by a back heel kick. Van Dam with a step over heel kick to Cesaro followed by Rolling Thunder to Van Dam. Del Rio with an arm breaker but he misses the step up enzuigiri. Van Dam with a split legged moonsault for a near fall. Ziggler tries for the hesitation DDT but Cesaro counters with a pop up European uppercut.

Van Dam with a kick to Cesaro that forces Cesaro to the floor. Van Dam goes up top for the Five Star Frog Splash but Del Rio gets his knees up and then he applies the cross arm breaker and Van Dam taps out.

Winners: Cesaro and Alberto Del Rio

After the match, Heyman joins Cesaro in the ring and then Del Rio says something to Cesaro and Heyman. Cesaro kicks Del Rio and hits the Gotch Style Neutralizer.

Renee Young is in the interview area with the Usos. She asks them about defending the titles on Sunday. Jimmy says that they are cockroaches or flies that you cannot get rid of at a barbecue. Jey says the Early Bird gets the worm. Jey says that they are bringing the whole arsenal on the 29th. Jimmy says that they will still be the tag team champions after Money in the Bank.
