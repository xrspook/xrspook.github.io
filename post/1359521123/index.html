THURSDAY! Don’t miss World Heavyweight Champion ALBERTO DEL RIO at WWE NXT at Full Sail Live!

by WWE NXT on Tuesday, January 29, 2013 at 11:18pm ·

Witness the World Heavyweight Champion, Alberto Del Rio, in the ring during the January 31, 2013 WWE NXT event at Full Sail Live! Also, witness history in the making, as the first ever NXT Tag Team Champions will be crowned as the NXT Tag Team Championship Tournament is sure to end with a bang! Alberto Del Rio’s personal ring announcer, Ricardo Rodriguez, will welcome the champ to the ring on a night that will also include appearances from all the NXT Superstars and the beautiful WWE Divas.

  • Doors open at 5:45 p.m.
  • Event begins at 6:30 p.m.
  • FREE to students, 2 tickets maximum, w/Full Sail I.D. (Limited quantity, - - while they last!)
  • $5.00 for Full Sail Staff w/Full Sail I.D.
  • $10.00 for General Admission tickets

Available at the Full Sail Live Box Office only: 141 University Park Drive, Winter Park, FL 32792

Jan. 29, 2013 from 11am to 1pm & 4pm to 6pm

Jan. 30-31, 2013 from 4pm to 6pm

Proceeds from ticket contributions benefit the Full Sail WWE Scholarship Fund.

Note:

No phone or email requests for tickets will be accepted.

Tickets and seating for this event will be made available on a first come, first serve basis.

Arrive before 6:30pm, all available seats will be offered to the standby line after the event has started.

WWE and Full Sail University will be videotaping this event. During the WWE / NXT event, a selection of local area specialty food trucks will be on site, so come hungry, as the Food Truck service begins at 5:00pm.

Future Event Tickets:

Tickets for the next scheduled WWE NXT live event will go on sale immediately following the conclusion of this event.

Event specific WWE NXT T-Shirts will be available at each event. Proceeds from t-shirt contributions benefit the Full Sail / WWE Scholarship Fund.
