ONGOING WWE RAW 1010 REPORT: HAPPY STEVE AUSTIN DAY WITH THE TITLE MR. PUNK; WHO WILL HELP AJ RUN RAW; THE GREATEST DEBATE THIS WEEK LATER TONIGHT; TONIGHT WE APPRECIATE JIM ROSS; TAG TOURNAMENT MATCH; WHAT IS THE FUTURE OF THE FUNK?

By Richard Trionfo on 2012-10-01 20:28:04

We begin this week’s show with a look back at Mick Foley’s comments to CM Punk and his attempt to convince Punk to accept a match against John Cena at Hell in a Cell. Then we see the stare down between Punk and Ryback.

We are live from Oklahoma City, Oklahoma and your announcers are Michael ‘Why aren’t there any Sonics in this town’ Cole and Jim ‘How much of this show will be seen on Smackdown’ Ross.

We see footage from after Smackdown when Alberto Del Rio attacked Randy Orton after Big Show knocked out Randy Orton at the end of Smackdown.

Ricardo joins the announcers for this match.

Match Number Eight: Alberto Del Rio (with Ricardo Rodriguez) versus Kofi Kingston (with R Truth)

Del Rio with a kick and suplex and he gets a near fall. Kofi with a leaping back elbow. Kofi clotheslines Alberto over the top rope to the floor. Kofi with a cross body off the turnbuckles onto Del Rio on the floor and we go to commercial.

We are back and Del Rio with an abdominal stretch. Del Rio applies extra pressure to the midsection but Kofi with a hip toss to escape the hold. Del Rio has a kick blocked and Kofi with chops followed by a drop kick. Kofi with a Superman punch and then he does a lap before setting for the Boom Drop and he hits it. Kofi sets for Trouble in Paradise but Del Rio ducks.

Del Rio misses a charge into the corner and Kofi with a pendulum kicks. Kofi misses a cross body and Del Rio kicks Kofi in the ribs and then he applies the cross arm breaker and Kofi tries to get to the ropes but he is unable to reach them and he has to tap out.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio
